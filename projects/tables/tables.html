LE SSERAFIM

Link Link Link Link

LE SSERAFIM is a 4th generation South Korean girl group under HYBE that debuted in May 2022. They are well known for their bold concept and choreography. Their name "LE SSERAFIM" is an anagram of the phrase "I'm fearless", emphasizing their theme of self-empowerment.
