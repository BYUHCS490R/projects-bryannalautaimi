What is normal flow in HTML?

Normal flow is the default layout of the content on a webpage.

What does float do? Why do you need to clear the float?

Float takes content out fo the normal flow and changes the positions. It needs to be cleared so that the content you choose can stay in the normal flow.

What do absolute, fixed, and relative do?

Absolute is when the content goes exactly where you tell it to go. Fixed is when it stays put in one place. Relative is kept in normal flow but you can change its position using a class.
