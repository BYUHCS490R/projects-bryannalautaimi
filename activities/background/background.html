Background Images

Question

When should you use a background image, and when should you use the IMG tag?

A background image should be used for decorative purposes, to make the webpage look nicer and more appealing. It doesn't usually serve a purpose, however. The IMG tag should be used when the photo is meant to be looked at by the viewers, as it is content of the webpage.
