The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do?

It is the path or the destination we want to go to when we press the submit button.

The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do?

It tells the browser how the data should be sent once submitted.

What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"?

GET shows the data in the URL while POST doesn't, making POST more secure.
