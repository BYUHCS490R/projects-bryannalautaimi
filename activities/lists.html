What are special entity characters?

They are code that is used to display symbols or characters that aren't on regular keyboards or when less than or greater than signs are needed to display without making an html tag, and they start with &.

    Examples
  • <
  • >
  • ©
  • €
  • £
  • ¥

What are phrase tags?

Tags that show the meaning of a block of text on a webpage.

What is the difference between OL and UL tags?

  1. OL stands for Ordered List. These kind of lists are numbered and shows the difference in what number element.
  • UL stands for Unordered List. THese kind of lists are bulletted and it doesn't matter what order the list elements are in.

What is a DL?

It stands for Discription/Definition List. Used when elements and definitions are needed to be put together without breaks.
