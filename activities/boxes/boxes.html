Questions

What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

Block tags are tags that cause line breaks and inline tags are tags that do not cause line breaks and only select parts within a line.

What is the margin, border, and padding?

The space outside of a block are the margins. The border is the line that goes around the block. The padding is the space between the content in the block and the border.

What are the units of measurement you can use in CSS?

px, cm, in, %, em, rem

When would you use a figure tag?

When you want to add a picture to your main content that may need a caption.
