What kind of photos are good to encode using JPEG?

Photos with good graphics and visuals, ones that have a lot of color and detail such as real-world photos like landscapes and portraits.

What kind of photos are good to encode using GIF?

Works best with pictures with not too many colors and easy to compress like animation, cartoons, and logos.

What is unique about the PNG format?

It has transparency, some of the pixels can be see-through
